# Office Directory Overview

This directory serves as a dedicated workspace for planning and managing the setup and growth of a freelance Out-of-Home (OOH) advertising business. The primary goal is to establish a competitive presence in the alternative OOH market, drawing insights from established players like All Points Media.

## Key Files

*   **`ooh_freelance_planner.md`**: This file contains a detailed, day-by-day planner outlining tasks and micro-goals for building the freelance OOH business. It includes steps for client and venue prospecting, as well as templates for outreach.

## Usage

This directory is intended to be used as a central hub for guiding the initial phases of your freelance OOH advertising venture. By following the structured plan in `ooh_freelance_planner.md`, you can systematically approach client acquisition, venue partnerships, and operational setup. The content will evolve as the business progresses, with new plans and resources being added for subsequent stages of development.
