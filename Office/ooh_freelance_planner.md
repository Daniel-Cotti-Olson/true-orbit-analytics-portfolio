# OOH Freelance Business Planner

This planner outlines your daily tasks and micro-goals to build your freelance Out-of-Home (OOH) advertising business, focusing on competing with established players like All Points Media.

---

## Day 1: Initial Prospecting & Outreach Preparation

**Goal for the day:** Identify key client contacts and potential local venues, and prepare personalized outreach messages.

**Morning (Focus: Client Identification & Value Proposition)**

1.  **8:00 AM - 9:00 AM: Review All Points Media's Testimonials & Client Types**
    *   **Action:** Go to `allpointsco.com` and thoroughly read the client testimonials.
    *   **Action:** List out the *types* of clients (e.g., daycare, sports team, national brand) and any specific client names mentioned.
    *   **Action:** Identify industries that seem to regularly use alternative OOH advertising.
    *   **Outcome:** A clearer understanding of APM's client base and industries to target.

2.  **9:00 AM - 10:30 AM: Identify Target Client Contacts (3-5)**
    *   **Your Action:**
        *   **For Mindshare, ESPN Productions, Inc., Metro by T-Mobile, and SUPERVALUE:**
            *   Go directly to **LinkedIn.com**.
            *   Search for each company.
            *   Navigate to the "People" tab on their company page.
            *   Use the search filters for "Title" (e.g., "Media Planner," "Media Buyer," "Associate Director, OOH," "Director, Integrated Media," "Marketing Manager," "Brand Manager") and "Keywords" (e.g., "Out of Home," "OOH").
            *   Filter for "Current" employees.
            *   Identify 1-2 primary contacts for each company.
        *   **For Kids First (Philadelphia):**
            *   Visit their website (search: "Kids First Philadelphia").
            *   Look for an "Executive Director," "Marketing Manager," or main contact on "Contact Us," "About Us," or "Team" pages. If not found, use LinkedIn with "Kids First Philadelphia" and "Director" or "Manager."
    *   **Outcome:** A list of 1-2 identified contact names and available contact methods (email or LinkedIn profile) for each of your 5 target clients.

3.  **10:30 AM - 12:00 PM: Draft Initial Client Outreach Message (Personalize Template)**
    *   **Action:** Use the template below and personalize it for each identified client contact.
    *   **Outcome:** 5 personalized client outreach messages ready for sending.

    ```
    Subject: Innovative Out-of-Home Advertising Partnership for [Client Company Name]

    Dear [Contact Person Name],

    My name is [Your Name], and I specialize in highly targeted and cost-effective Out-of-Home (OOH) advertising solutions. I'm reaching out because I've been following [Client Company Name]'s [mention a recent campaign, initiative, or their work in general if you can find one, otherwise keep it general, e.g., "innovative marketing efforts"] and believe there's a unique opportunity to amplify your message through alternative OOH channels.

    My approach offers the agility and personalized service of a dedicated freelance expert, with a significantly more efficient cost structure than traditional agencies. This allows me to deliver exceptional value and impactful campaigns tailored specifically to your objectives, whether it's enhancing brand presence, driving local traffic, or reaching niche demographics.

    I'd be keen to schedule a brief, 15-20 minute virtual coffee to discuss your current marketing goals and explore how a strategic OOH placement could provide a fresh, budget-friendly avenue to engage your target audience. Would [suggest a specific day/time range] work for you next week, or please let me know what time is best?

    Thank you for your time and consideration.

    Sincerely,

    [Your Name]
    [Your Title/Freelance Service Name]
    [Your Contact Information/LinkedIn Profile URL]
    ```

**Afternoon (Focus: Venue Identification & Partnership Preparation)**

4.  **1:00 PM - 2:30 PM: Identify Local "Place Based Media" Venues (2-3)**
    *   **Action:** Think about your immediate local area.
    *   **Action:** Identify 2-3 specific businesses that fit the categories All Points Media targets (e.g., a popular independent coffee shop, a busy local gym, a well-frequented barber shop/salon, a local community center with high foot traffic).
    *   **Action:** Note their names, addresses, and find a primary contact number or email (usually via their website).
    *   **Outcome:** A list of 2-3 specific local venue names and primary contact methods.

5.  **2:30 PM - 4:30 PM: Draft Initial Venue Outreach Message (Personalize Template)**
    *   **Action:** Use the template below and personalize it for each identified venue contact.
    *   **Outcome:** 2-3 personalized venue outreach messages ready for sending.

    ```
    Subject: Partnership Opportunity: Host Local Advertising at [Venue Name]

    Dear [Venue Owner/Manager Name, or "Business Owner"],

    My name is [Your Name], and I'm an independent specialist in connecting local businesses with highly visible advertising opportunities in unique locations like yours. I'm reaching out because [Venue Name] has a fantastic [describe characteristic, e.g., "foot traffic," "local clientele," "community presence"] and I believe it would be an ideal spot for targeted Out-of-Home advertising.

    I work with various local and national brands looking to reach specific audiences. Partnering with me to host advertising in your space could provide you with a new, consistent revenue stream. I handle all the logistics, from matching advertisers to managing the placement, ensuring minimal disruption to your operations.

    I'd love to chat briefly about how this could work for you, including potential revenue sharing or even complimentary advertising for your own business. Would you be open to a quick 10-15 minute call sometime this week?

    Thank you for your time.

    Sincerely,

    [Your Name]
    [Your Title/Freelance Service Name]
    [Your Contact Information]
    ```

**End of Day (Review)**

6.  **4:30 PM - 5:00 PM: Final Review**
    *   **Action:** Review all identified contacts and drafted messages.
    *   **Outcome:** All preparations complete for actual outreach.
