---
A Note on Mug Designs:

Mugs offer a larger canvas than hats and can even feature "wraparound" designs that tell a story as you turn the mug. You can use slightly more detailed designs here, but clean, bold graphics still tend to look best.

---

Positive Prompt:

Negative Prompt:
