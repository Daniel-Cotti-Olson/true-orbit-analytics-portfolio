# Project Plan: The YouTube & SEO Marketing Engine

This document outlines the complete business strategy and action plan for the "YouTube Producer / SEO Marketer" project.

## 1. Core Strategy: The "Video Resource Hub"

The fundamental goal is to build a high-authority brand by combining YouTube video production with a dedicated, fast-loading website. This creates a symbiotic relationship:

1.  **High-quality videos** are created and published on a YouTube channel.
2.  For each key video, a dedicated **"resource page"** is created on our own website.
3.  This page contains the embedded video PLUS a wealth of text-based content that search engines love: a full transcript, detailed show notes, images, and links to resources.
4.  These content-rich pages rank on Google, driving organic traffic to our brand's website.
5.  Visitors to the website watch the video, increasing the YouTube channel's views, watch time, and authority, which in turn helps it grow.
6.  The website captures leads via newsletters and other calls-to-action, building a direct relationship with the audience.

## 2. Business Goal: Sponsorship Readiness

The primary monetization goal is to attract high-quality sponsors to the YouTube channel. We defined a "Sponsorship-Ready" brand as one that meets these milestones:

*   **Subscribers:** > 10,000
*   **Audience:** > 2,000 consistent views per video (within the first 30 days).
*   **Website Authority:** > 5,000 monthly unique visitors to the resource hub pages.
*   **Professionalism:** A polished, consistent brand identity.

## 3. Projected Timeline

*   **8-10 months:** The target timeframe to become attractive to smaller, niche-specific sponsors.
*   **12-18 months:** The target timeframe to become attractive to larger, more established brands.
