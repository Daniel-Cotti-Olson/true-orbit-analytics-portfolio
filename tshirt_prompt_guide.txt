### T-Shirt Design & Prompt Guide

Here is a guide to help you create new, best-selling t-shirt designs.

### Analysis of Your Current Design

Your current prompt, "A majestic wolf howling at a geometric moon, vintage, distressed texture, t-shirt graphic design, vector, single color, transparent background," is excellent. It aligns perfectly with two major trends: **Vintage/Retro** aesthetics and **Nature/Animal** themes. The "vector" and "single color" keywords are ideal for creating clean, print-ready graphics.

### Front vs. Back Placement: What Sells Best?

Regarding image placement, you have two strong options that are popular in the market:

1.  **Front Only (The Classic):** This is the most common and timeless approach. A single, well-placed design on the front of the shirt is a guaranteed winner and appeals to the broadest audience.
2.  **Large Back Graphic + Small Front Graphic (The Trend):** A very popular modern style is to feature a large, bold, or intricate design covering most of the back of the shirt. This is often paired with a small, simple logo or a small element from the main design on the front, typically over the left chest area.

**Recommendation:** Avoid placing the same large graphic on both the front and back, as it can be visually overwhelming and is not a common design practice. For a start, focusing on strong, single front-prints is most efficient.

### Prompt Guide for Best-Selling T-Shirt Designs

Here are three alternative design prompts based on current best-sellers. You can copy and paste these directly into the positive and negative prompt fields in your workflow.

#### Design 1: Minimalist Line Art

This style is clean, modern, and very popular. It relies on simple, elegant lines to create a striking image.

**Positive Prompt:**
```

**Negative Prompt:**
```
