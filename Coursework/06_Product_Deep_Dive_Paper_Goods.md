# Module 6: Product Deep Dive: Paper Goods

In this module, we'll explore creating designs specifically for paper products. These items have unique requirements for layout, style, and dimension. We will continue with our "Enchanted Forest Creatures" theme to generate our examples.

---

## 1. Children's Coloring Book Pages

Coloring books require simple, clear line art with distinct areas to color. The designs should be engaging and tell a story. We will generate a standard portrait-oriented page.

**Key Style Prompts:** `coloring book page, black and white, thick clean lines, no shading, whimsical, for kids`

**EmptyLatentImage Dimensions:**
*   `width`: 816
*   `height`: 1056
*(This corresponds to a standard 8.5"x11" page at 96 DPI, which scales perfectly to 300 DPI for printing: 2550x3300 pixels)*

---
### Example Prompt:

**Positive Prompt:**
`coloring book page of a friendly fox character wearing a tiny backpack, surrounded by mushrooms and ferns in an enchanted forest, black and white, thick clean lines, no shading, whimsical, for kids, simple, bold illustration`

**Negative Prompt:**
`photorealistic, color, shading, gradients, complex details, scary, text, signature, watermark, blurry`

---

## 2. Wall Calendar Images

Calendar images are typically landscape-oriented and need to be visually rich to be interesting for a full month. We can generate a full scene.

**Key Style Prompts:** `digital painting, vibrant colors, beautiful lighting, detailed, storybook illustration`

**EmptyLatentImage Dimensions:**
*   `width`: 1024
*   `height`: 768
*(A standard 4:3 landscape ratio that can be easily cropped or scaled for typical calendar layouts.)*

---
### Example Prompt:

**Positive Prompt:**
`digital painting of a wise old owl reading a book on a branch, fireflies lighting the scene, in an enchanted forest at twilight, vibrant colors, beautiful lighting, detailed, storybook illustration`

**Negative Prompt:**
`black and white, human, text, signature, watermark, blurry, ugly, deformed`

---

## 3. Greeting Cards (Image Only)

Greeting cards are often portrait-oriented. The key is a single, impactful image that conveys a feeling or occasion. We will focus on creating the cover art.

**Key Style Prompts:** `isolated on white background, charming character illustration, watercolor style, soft colors`

**EmptyLatentImage Dimensions:**
*   `width`: 720
*   `height`: 1080
*(A 2:3 portrait ratio, perfect for a standard 5"x7.5" greeting card cover.)*

---
### Example Prompt:

**Positive Prompt:**
`charming character illustration of a baby hedgehog holding a single flower, isolated on white background, watercolor style, soft colors, gentle, cute, simple`

**Negative Prompt:**
`photorealistic, dark, scary, complex background, text, signature, watermark, ugly, deformed`
