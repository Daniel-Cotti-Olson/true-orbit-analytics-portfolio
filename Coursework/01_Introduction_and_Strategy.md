# Module 1: Introduction & The Multi-Stream Strategy

## Welcome!

Welcome to the first step in your journey to building a successful, AI-powered creative business. My name is Ethan Harrington, and I'll be your instructor and guide through this process.

The goal of this course is not just to teach you how to create AI art, but to teach you how to think like a creative entrepreneur. We will build a business from the ground up, focusing on creating multiple streams of income to build a resilient and profitable brand.

---

## The "Multiple Streams of Income" Philosophy

Why not just sell one thing? In the fast-moving world of digital creation, relying on a single product or platform is risky. Our strategy is to diversify from day one. We will focus on three core paths:

1.  **AI-Generated Products:** This is our primary creative outlet. We'll create beautiful and unique designs and sell them as both digital downloads and on physical Print-on-Demand (POD) products.
2.  **Creator Tools (The "Picks and Shovels" Model):** While others are "mining for gold" by selling designs, we'll also sell them the tools they need to succeed, like high-quality product mockups and templates. This is a powerful B2B (Business-to-Business) model.
3.  **Informational Products:** As we learn, we will document our process. Every challenge we overcome and every success we achieve is a lesson. We will package these lessons into a valuable course (the one you're reading now!) to sell to others who want to follow in our footsteps.

---

## Core Technologies We Will Use

*   **AI Image Generation (ComfyUI):** Our creative engine. We will use this powerful, node-based tool to create all of our artwork.
*   **E-commerce Platforms (Etsy & Shopify):** Our digital storefronts where we will list and sell our products.
*   **Print-on-Demand (POD) Services (Printify, Printful):** Our automated fulfillment partners who will handle the printing, packing, and shipping of our physical products.
