## Part 5: FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

**1. What kind of data file do you need?**
> We can work with most common formats! A simple Excel (`.xlsx`) or CSV (`.csv`) file is perfect. You can typically export this directly from your sales platform (like Shopify, Etsy, Stripe, QuickBooks, etc.). If you're not sure how, we can provide simple instructions after you order.

**2. Is my data secure with you?**
> Absolutely. We treat all client data with the highest level of confidentiality and professionalism. Your data will only be used to create your report and will not be shared with any third parties. We are committed to your privacy and security.

**3. What if I don't understand the report?**
> Our reports are designed to be as clear and easy to understand as possible. We avoid jargon and focus on what the numbers mean for your business. For our Premium package clients, we also include a 15-minute video call to walk you through the findings and answer any questions you have.

**4. Can you analyze data from a specific platform (e.g., Amazon, WooCommerce)?**
> Yes. As long as you can export your sales data into a spreadsheet format (like CSV or Excel), we can analyze it. Our methods are platform-agnostic.

**5. What's the difference between a "recommendation" and a "strategic plan"?**
> A **recommendation** is a specific, actionable insight based on the data (e.g., "Product X is your most profitable item; consider promoting it more heavily"). A **strategic plan** (included in the Premium package) is a more comprehensive, forward-looking document that ties multiple recommendations together into a cohesive growth strategy for your business.
