# Business Status & Strategy

This document tracks the current status, goals, and next actions for our AI-powered creative business.

## Vision
To build a multi-faceted creative business with several streams of income, including:
1.  **AI-Generated Products:** Selling unique artwork on digital and physical goods (stickers, apparel, etc.).
2.  **Creator Tools:** Selling "picks and shovels" like mockups and templates to other creators.
3.  **Informational Products:** Monetizing the knowledge gained through this process via courses or e-books.

## Current Phase
**Phase 1: Research & Portfolio Development**

## Immediate Goal
To identify the most profitable product categories for a beginner and create a foundational portfolio of high-quality designs for these categories.

## Next Actions
1.  **Analyze Top-Selling Products:** Research and identify the top 3 most profitable/highest-volume product categories for a beginner in the POD and digital product space.
2.  **Create Starter Workflows:** Develop baseline ComfyUI workflows for each of the top 3 product categories.
3.  **Draft First Course Module:** Create the introductory module for our future "how-to" course.
