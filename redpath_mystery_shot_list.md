# Shot List: The Redpath Mansion Mystery

This document provides visual suggestions to accompany the voice-over script for "The Redpath Mansion Mystery." The visual style should be dark, mysterious, and use a "Ken Burns" effect (slow panning and zooming) on historical photos to create a sense of life and motion.
