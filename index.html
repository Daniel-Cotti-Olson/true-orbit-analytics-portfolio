Case Study: Project "Clarity"

Automated E-commerce Sales Reporting

The Challenge: A growing e-commerce store was "drowning in data." They had thousands of sales records but no easy way to understand their performance week-to-week.

Our Solution: We developed a Python script, "Project Clarity," that automatically ingests their weekly sales CSV. The tool performs a full analysis, identifies best and worst-selling products, tracks revenue trends, and generates a professional, multi-page PDF report with charts and an AI-written executive summary.

The Result: The business owner now receives a clear, actionable intelligence report in their inbox every Monday morning, allowing them to make informed decisions on inventory, marketing, and strategy in minutes, not hours.
