# Project Plan: Multiple Income Streams with AI

This document outlines the strategy and action items for building multiple income streams using AI-generated content.

## Path A: Selling Tools for Creators (The "Picks and Shovels" Model)

### Objective
To replicate the success of shops like "CUOnline" by creating and selling high-quality digital assets that other print-on-demand and Etsy sellers need.

### Strategy
1.  Identify the most in-demand mockup and seller tool categories (e.g., popular t-shirt brands, trending items).
2.  Create clean, professional, and easy-to-use mockups and charts.
3.  Package these assets for digital download.

### Action Items
- [ ] **Decision:** Choose the first mockup type to create (e.g., Bella Canvas 3001 T-shirt, Gildan 18000 sweatshirt, a specific hat style).
- [ ] **Production:** Plan the process for creating the mockup (e.g., finding a suitable base image, preparing it for design overlays).

## Path B: Selling AI-Generated Art & Designs

### Objective
To create and sell unique, appealing AI-generated artwork on platforms like Etsy and Shopify as digital downloads and for print-on-demand products (stickers, t-shirts, mugs, etc.).

### Strategy
1.  Research popular niches, themes, and artistic styles that are currently selling well.
2.  Develop a unique artistic direction that blends market trends with your personal vision.
3.  Generate high-quality artwork using ComfyUI.
4.  Prepare the artwork for production (e.g., upscaling, background removal, formatting for specific products).

### Market Research Findings
- **Tech & "Meta" Themes:** Designs related to AI itself, cyberpunk culture, coding humor, and "pro-human artist" messages are popular.
- **Cute / "Kawaii" Style:** A very popular category, often featuring cute animals (raccoons, cats, corgis) and anime-inspired characters.
- **Nature & Scenery:** Stylized landscapes, forests, and mountains sell well.
- **Common Artistic Styles:** Watercolor effects and clean, vector-like graphics are prevalent. Holographic sticker variations are also a popular product feature.

### Action Items
- [x] **Market Research:** Identify top-selling themes and styles for AI-generated stickers and POD products.
- [ ] **Creative Direction:** Based on the research, define the first design series to create. (***Next Step***)
